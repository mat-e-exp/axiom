Regime Coverage

What Axiom covers across the insurance regulatory landscape.

Comprehensive support for UK and EU insurance regulatory reporting obligations, from Solvency UK to Lloyd's market returns.

PRA / Bank of England
Solvency UK
Quarterly and annual Pillar III returns, SCR calculations, ORSA, SFCR, and technical provisions. Handles Solvency UK XBRL taxonomy requirements and tracks PRA deadline changes as published.
Managing Agents & Syndicates
Lloyd's Market
QF1 quarterly financial returns, DyGIST returns, overseas trust fund adjustments, LCR/SCR capital submissions, syndicate annual accounts. Manages the Lloyd's business timetable, automates MD5 checksum compliance, and enforces director sign-off.
Consumer & Wholesale
FCA Conduct
RegData submissions, complaints reporting, Consumer Duty attestations, and product governance returns. Maintains the data lineage and sign-off records that FCA supervisors expect in a review.
Dual-Regime Firms
EIOPA / EU Solvency II
For firms under both Solvency UK and EU Solvency II, manages parallel reporting obligations — tracking the EU's January 2027 implementation deadline and maintaining regime-specific audit trails without duplicating effort.
