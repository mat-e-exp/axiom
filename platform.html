The Platform

Purpose-built for deadline-critical regulated workflows.

Axiom acts as the governance and orchestration layer across your regulatory return process — automating data collection, enforcing submission rules, managing approval workflows, and providing a complete audit trail from source data to filed return.

Policy Engine
Encodes all submission rules, deadline calendars, regulatory templates, and territory-specific requirements as deterministic policy. The system knows what is due, when, in what format, and from which source.
Workflow Auditability
Every data pull, transformation, validation check, exception flag, and sign-off is logged with full timestamp and user attribution. The complete evidence chain is available on demand.
Agent Orchestration
AI agents collect data from source systems, run validation against regulatory schemas, flag exceptions for human review, and prepare draft returns. Teams review exceptions — not build from scratch.
Human-in-the-Loop
Board-level sign-off gates are built into every workflow. No return submits without required approvals. Records are timestamped, attributed, and retained for Lloyd's director sign-off requirements.
Enterprise Memory
Prior period returns, methodology decisions, and assumption sets are retained in context — enabling year-on-year consistency checks and providing the continuity evidence regulators expect.
Regulatory Change Absorption
As the PRA and Lloyd's publish updated requirements, Axiom's policy layer is updated centrally — not firm by firm. Template versions, taxonomy changes, and deadline shifts propagate automatically.
